Multi-agent AI research system powered by CrewAI

How It Works

This project uses CrewAI to orchestrate two AI agents that work together sequentially. Enter a research topic, and the agents collaborate to produce a ranked college report.

AI Agents

Researcher
High School Student Researching Colleges
Searches the web for top colleges offering strong Computer Science programs. Investigates rankings, admission requirements, faculty quality, research opportunities, internship pipelines, and CS specialties like AI, cybersecurity, and data science.
Reporting Analyst
College Rankings Analyst
Takes the research findings and organizes colleges into a ranked report grouped by tier (Top Tier, Strong, Up-and-Coming). Highlights which CS subjects each school excels in, making it easy to compare options at a glance.

Tasks

Research Task
Conduct thorough research on the given topic. Find top colleges with strong CS programs, including rankings, CS specialties, notable strengths, and standout features.
Reporting Task
Review all research findings and produce a concise ranked report organized by tier, with each entry showing rank, CS subject strengths, and a summary of why it stands out.
